ℹ️ How To Use
Browsing Pokémon
Search by name or Pokédex number. Use the 🔍 Filter button to narrow by format rating, type, generation, flags, and more.

Watchlist ⭐
Add any Pokémon to your personal watchlist in two ways:
• Tap the ☆ star in the bottom-right corner of their card on the main page
• Or tap ☆ Watchlist at the top of their individual page
Access your watchlist anytime with the ⭐ Watchlist button in the top bar.

Ratings
Each Pokémon has community ratings for Raids, Great League, Ultra League, Master League, and Gym Defense. Ratings go from S (best) to E (bottom tier). Unrated means no data is available yet.

Evolution Arrow ↑
A green ↑ arrow on a card means this Pokémon evolves into something worth having.

⚠️ Flag
A ⚠️ badge means there are other factors that may make this Pokémon worth catching — such as Little Cup viability or special moves.

Suggest a Rating Change
On any Pokémon's detail page, tap 💬 Suggest a Rating Change to propose a different tier or suggest a note to add.
💬 Feedback
Found a bug or have a suggestion? Let us know!
Feedback is saved locally on your device.
